Our Story

Redefining the standard of luxury automotive care since 2018.

Our Mission

To deliver uncompromising, high-end car restoration using advanced waterless technology. Born from the need to eliminate wasteful wash practices, we bring concierge-level detailing to the world’s most elite vehicles.

We pride ourselves on a proprietary polymer formula that lifting dirt safely while preserving millions of gallons of water annually. Our commitment is perfection without compromise.

Our Vision

To lead the global transition toward sustainable vehicle maintenance, proving that the deepest showroom shine can be achieved with zero environmental impact.

We envision a future where luxury car care is defined by technological innovation, environmental stewardship, and the preservation of our planet's most precious resources, one masterpiece at a time.

The Spark Of Innovation

It started in 2024. Watching traditional car washes waste up to 200 liters of water per vehicle felt fundamentally disconnected from the future. We knew there was a better way to achieve a showroom finish.

Collaborating with top-tier chemists, we pioneered a polymer-based restoration method that lifts dirt safely away from the paint, encapsulating it for a scratch-free removal. No hoses, no runoff, just perfection.

Today, EcoShine is the trusted concierge for luxury fleet owners who demand the best for their cars and the earth.
